About Us

Pizza Man was established in 2020 with the mission to provide the best pizza in town. Our vision is to become the leading pizza delivery service in the region.

Brief History

Pizzaman Chickenman is Ghana’s fastest-growing fast-food chain, specializing in pizzas, grilled chicken, rice dishes, and other fast-food meals. The brand started in 2018 in Kumasi and quickly expanded across Accra, Kumasi, and other cities. Known for its affordable pricing, large meal portions, and innovative menu, Pizzaman Chickenman has become a household name in Ghana. The company operates multiple branches and provides delivery services via platforms like Bolt Food and Glovo.

Vision

To become Ghana’s No.1 fast-food brand by offering high-quality, delicious meals at affordable prices with excellent customer service.

Mission

  • To provide Ghanaians with accessible and tasty fast food.
  • To expand operations across Africa, making Pizzaman Chickenman an international franchise.
  • To improve food delivery services with an efficient digital ordering system.

Achievements

Opened 20 branches across Ghana within 3 years.

Recognized as the fastest-growing fast-food chain in Ghana.

Awarded Best Customer Service in the fast-food industry in 2021.

Partnered with major delivery platforms like Bolt Food and Glovo.
